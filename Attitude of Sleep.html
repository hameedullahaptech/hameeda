Attitude of Sleep

About the Teacher


Jim Bennitt


Jim Bennitt—a former wrestler, massage therapist, and clerk at the Chicago Board of Trade—began his studies in yoga, tantra, and ayurveda in 1997. He originally took up yoga as a way to help heal back pain, but soon after, discovered how the practice affects not only the body but also the mind and spirit. Jim has completed teacher trainings with Daren Friesen of Moksha Yoga Center, which draws heavily from the Krishnamacharya lineage, apprenticed with Gabriel Halpern of the Yoga Circle (a studio in the Iyengar tradition), and spent two months in Kiev with Ukrainian yoga master Andre Lappa for an intensive in Universal Yoga. In 2002 he began a close apprenticeship with Rod Stryker, founder of ParaYoga, in the Himalayan tradition. Jim has been featured in such publications as The New York Times.
